Private label method

From CasaFolino range to your shelf-ready brand

CasaFolino is not a simple catalog supplier. We help professional buyers turn Mediterranean products into a structured private label project, ready for retail, distribution, horeca or e-commerce channels.

What we do

We start from proven CasaFolino product families and adapt assortment, formats, packaging direction, commercial documentation and launch priorities around the buyer's target market.

How we work

The process is concrete: product selection, feasibility check, label and packaging requirements, technical documentation, sample alignment, production planning and commercial follow-up.

Why it matters

A buyer receives more than jars and cartons: they receive a coherent Mediterranean range with traceability, food-safety culture, export mindset and practical support before and after the first order.

Custom recipes

Product development with a clear commercial purpose

When the standard range is not enough, CasaFolino can work on a custom recipe: a flavor profile, ingredient combination or product concept designed around the client's positioning, channel and expected consumer use.

What we can customize

Taste intensity, ingredient combinations, texture, sweetness or savory balance, pairing logic, serving occasion, format and packaging direction can be discussed project by project.

How we protect feasibility

A custom recipe must remain producible, stable, documentable and commercially understandable. We keep creativity tied to operational reality, lead times and food-safety requirements.

Who it is for

The service is useful for private label brands, gourmet retailers, importers, distributors and food-service projects that need a differentiated product without losing industrial discipline.
