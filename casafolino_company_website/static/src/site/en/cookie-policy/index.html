What are cookies

Cookies and local storage

Cookies are small text files a website stores on your device. This site also uses local storage for the same purpose (remembering your cookie choice). We use them to run the site correctly and, only with your consent, to measure traffic and improve our content for B2B buyers, importers and distributors.

Cookies we use

Strictly necessary (always active, no consent required)

cf_consent

First-party, local storage · 12 months

Stores your cookie choice (accepted/rejected) so we don't ask again on every visit.

Analytics (only after you accept)

_ga, _ga_*

Google Analytics 4 · up to 13 months

Distinguishes visitors and sessions to measure how the catalog and site pages are used. Provider: Google Ireland Ltd.

Google Tag Manager

Tag delivery · session

Loads the analytics tag itself. Not yet active — reserved for future marketing tags, always gated by the same consent choice.

Your choice

On your first visit a banner asks you to Accept or Reject analytics cookies. Rejecting still lets you browse and use the catalog, request tech sheets and submit business inquiries normally — only traffic measurement is disabled. You can change your mind at any time with the Manage cookie preferences button above, or in the footer of every page.

Legal basis: consent (art. 6.1.a GDPR) for analytics cookies; legitimate interest for strictly necessary storage needed to run the site. This applies to visitors in the EU/UK and, as a matter of policy, to all visitors regardless of location.

For any question about this policy or your data, see our Privacy Policy or contact info@casafolino.com.
