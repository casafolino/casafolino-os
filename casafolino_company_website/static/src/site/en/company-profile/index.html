Who we are

An Italian family company, built for international partners

CasaFolino is a family-owned Italian food company, founded in 1962 in Calabria, in the heart of the Mediterranean. After six decades of food experience, the company was relaunched with a clear mission: to bring authentic Italian Mediterranean products to professional markets across the world.

We select high-quality ingredients, work with certified production processes and develop practical, distinctive products for retailers, distributors, ho.re.ca. and private label partners. Italian gastronomic culture meets structured operations, reliable documentation and scalable production planning.

From spreadable creams and flavored honeys to ready risottos and chocolate, every CasaFolino product carries the same promise: real Made in Italy quality, ready for international shelves.
