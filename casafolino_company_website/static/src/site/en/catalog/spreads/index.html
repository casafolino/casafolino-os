Catalog category

Spreads

Classic and bold spreadable creams for breakfast, pastry and gourmet retail.

CasaFolino produces premium Italian spreadable creams for retail shelves, pastry professionals and gourmet food projects. Our range combines classic favourites and bold Mediterranean recipes, from rich pistachio cream to hazelnut and distinctive gourmet spreads, all crafted with selected ingredients in our certified Italian facility.

For distributors and retailers, our spreads are a high-rotation, high-margin category with strong shelf impact. For private label partners, we develop tailor-made recipes, formats and packaging, with documentation ready for supplier qualification and import.

Ideal for: gourmet retail, breakfast and pastry, gift sets, ho.re.ca. and private label ranges.
