Flavored honeys

Flavored honeys for cheese pairings, teas, desserts and natural sweet applications.

CasaFolino is an Italian producer of flavored and infused honeys, designed for cheese pairings, gourmet retail, tea and dessert applications. Our Mediterranean honey range turns a traditional product into a distinctive, premium-priced specialty for buyers who need shelf differentiation.

We supply flavored honey for branded retail, ho.re.ca. and private label projects, with the flexibility to develop custom flavours and formats. Every batch is fully traceable and supported by certified documentation, making CasaFolino a reliable honey supplier for distributors and importers worldwide.

Ideal for: cheese counters and delis, gourmet retail, hospitality, gift and tasting sets, private label honey ranges.
