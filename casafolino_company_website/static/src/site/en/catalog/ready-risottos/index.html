Ready risottos

Ready risottos with Sibari rice and Italian recipes for quick high-quality dishes.

CasaFolino produces authentic Italian ready risottos, made with Sibari rice and traditional recipes that deliver a high-quality dish in minutes. For retailers and distributors, ready risotto is a convenience category that combines genuine Made in Italy appeal with everyday practicality.

Our risottos are available for branded supply and private label development, with the option to adapt recipes, formats and packaging to your market. Long shelf life, certified production and complete traceability make CasaFolino a dependable risotto manufacturer for international B2B partners.

Ideal for: retail convenience shelves, gourmet and specialty stores, export ranges, private label projects.
