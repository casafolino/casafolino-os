  1. May 29-Jun 2, 2026Milan, Italy

    Europe · 2026

    Artigiano in Fiera Summer Preview

    CasaFolino announced its return to Milan for Artigiano in Fiera - Summer Preview at Fieramilano Rho, Hall 02, Stand B128, together with the Calabria Straordinaria regional collective.

    The event moves the story from international buyer meetings to direct tasting: honeys, mousses, spreadable creams and the new Lemon Drinking Cream, Pistachio Drinking Cream and Don Genna' Babà with Pistachio Cream presented to visitors as concrete Mediterranean taste experiences.

    Consumer tastingMilanProduct launchCalabria Straordinaria
  2. May 11-14, 2026Milan, Italy

    Europe · 2026

    TUTTOFOOD Milano 2026

    CasaFolino returned to Italy after a three-stop international route across Vancouver, Madrid and Montreal. The company presented the full Mediterranean range at two booths: Hall 4, Stand N06 and Hall 10, Stand K11, Position 55, within the Calabria Straordinaria collective.

    Focus products included the award-winning honey line, Crispy Chili, Crunchy Spreadable Creams, traditional risottos, Mediterranean mousses, spice blends, biscotti and chocolates. The fair also hosted the world premiere of Lemon Drinking Cream, Pistachio Drinking Cream and Don Genna' Babà with Pistachio Cream.

    RetailImportersPrivate labelProduct launch
  3. Apr 29-May 1, 2026Montreal, Canada

    North America · 2026

    SIAL Canada 2026

    CasaFolino joined SIAL Canada at the Palais des Congrès in Montreal, meeting buyers, importers, distributors and food professionals from North America and beyond. The company exhibited with the official Calabria Straordinaria collective at Booth 515.03.

    The White Chocolate Spreadable Cream with Crunchy Berries was selected for the official SIAL Innovation Selection 2026. CasaFolino presented the Crunchy Spreadable Creams line alongside honeys, Crispy Chili, risottos, mousses, spice blends and chocolates.

    Innovation selectionNorth AmericaDistribution
  4. Apr 21-24, 2026Madrid, Spain

    Europe · 2026

    Salón Gourmets Madrid

    CasaFolino participated in Salón Gourmets Madrid, one of Europe's reference events for specialty food. During the Madrid stop, the CasaFolino honey line received an international product recognition in the Salón Gourmets Awards.

    The event strengthened the positioning of the CasaFolino Mediterranean range for specialty retail, gourmet distribution, import channels and private label conversations.

    Specialty foodAward recognitionGourmet retail
  5. Apr 21-22, 2026Vancouver, Canada

    North America · 2026

    Grocery & Specialty Food West 2026

    CasaFolino opened its April 2026 international route in Vancouver at Grocery & Specialty Food West, presenting the Mediterranean range at the Italia Official Pavilion by ITA - Italian Trade Agency.

    The Vancouver stop focused on retailers, importers and distributors across Western Canada and the U.S. Pacific Northwest.

    CanadaITA pavilionRetail buyers
  6. 2025Vancouver, Canada

    North America · 2025

    Grocery & Specialty Food West 2025

    CasaFolino's LinkedIn update described the Vancouver 2025 participation as an important experience for meeting importers, building collaborations and sharing Calabrian excellence in Canada with the support of ICE - Italian Trade Agency.

    This earlier North American fair activity helped prepare the company's later trade fair route and strengthened commercial conversations around Made in Italy food products.
